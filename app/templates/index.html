Villagers & Heroes Utilities

This website contains a series of tools to supplement your gaming experience in Villagers & Heroes.

Crafting
Allows you to calculate the items, refined ingredients, and crafting components you will need to make recipes. You can select the recipes and quantities to craft in either the Equipment or Recipes tool.

Equipment
Displays the crafted equipment (armor, weapons, potions, etc.) by level for the class of your choosing. From this tool, you can select equipment to make and then utilize the Crafting tool to calculate the necessary resources.

Recipes
A searchable listing of all of the crafting recipes in the game. You can search and filter for the recipes you would like to make then utilize the Crafting tool to calculate the necessary resources.
